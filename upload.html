This is a web app for calculating intermediate interactors shared between proteins in the Biogrid database
by Ben Broderick Phillips

To start, upload two or more .tab2 files downloaded from Biogrid. Make sure to type in the correct protein name that each file corresponds to (e.g. HSP82), otherwise the app will not work, or you will get inaccurate results. When you've uploaded all the files, click "Find common interactions" to get your results. Depending on how many files you have uploaded, and how large they are, it may take some time. Be patient, and do good science.
